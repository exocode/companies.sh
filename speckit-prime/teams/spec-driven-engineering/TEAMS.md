The Spec-Driven Engineering team owns every phase of the build pipeline under
the CTO's direction. They receive a clarified spec and checklists from the CEO
(via the Spec Analyst) and return implemented, QA-reviewed code.

## Team Contract

- The CTO owns the pipeline; all escalations within the team route through the CTO.
- The CTO escalates unconvergeable blockers to the CEO.
- No team member contacts the human directly.
- Spec-Kit scripts and artifact paths are always preferred over manual inference
  (see `speckit-artifact-script-contract`).
